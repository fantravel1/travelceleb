Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about travel activities, planning your next adventure, and choosing the right experiences.

What are the most popular travel activities worldwide?
The most popular travel activities among creators and travelers include scuba diving and snorkeling (Great Barrier Reef, Maldives), mountain trekking (Everest Base Camp, Inca Trail), street food tours (Bangkok, Tokyo, Mexico City), wildlife safaris (Serengeti, Masai Mara), surfing (Bali, Hawaii), and cultural temple tours (Angkor Wat, Kyoto). Adventure activities and culinary experiences consistently rank as the most-filmed and most-searched travel content globally.
What luxury hotels do travel influencers feature most?
The most frequently featured luxury hotels by travel influencers include Soneva Fushi in the Maldives ($2,500+/night with 200+ creator features), Four Seasons Bali at Sayan ($800+/night with 300+ creator features), Aman Tokyo ($1,200+/night), Giraffe Manor in Nairobi ($1,500+/night for its unique wildlife experience), Ice Hotel in Sweden ($500+/night for seasonal Arctic luxury), and Marina Bay Sands in Singapore ($600+/night for its iconic infinity pool). These properties are popular because they offer unique, visually stunning, and share-worthy experiences.
Where are the best places for adventure travel?
The best adventure travel destinations include Queenstown, New Zealand for bungee jumping and skydiving; Interlaken, Switzerland for paragliding over the Alps; the Colorado River for white water rafting through the Grand Canyon; Railay Beach, Thailand for world-class rock climbing; the Great Barrier Reef, Australia for scuba diving; and Nepal for trekking to Everest Base Camp. Each destination has been extensively documented by adventure travel creators and offers professional outfitters with strong safety records.
What are the best food travel destinations?
The best food travel destinations include Bangkok, Thailand for its world-renowned street food scene (famously documented by Mark Wiens); Tokyo, Japan for sushi, ramen, and izakaya culture; Mexico City and Oaxaca for tacos and mole traditions; Naples, Bologna, and Rome in Italy for pizza and pasta; Delhi and Mumbai in India for explosive street food flavors; and Marrakech and Fez in Morocco for tagine and spice market experiences. Food travel is one of the fastest-growing niches in travel content creation.
How can I plan a wildlife encounter trip?
To plan a wildlife encounter trip, consider African safaris in the Serengeti or Masai Mara (visit June through October for the Great Migration), swimming with whale sharks in Mexico (June to September) or the Philippines (November to June), gorilla trekking in Uganda or Rwanda (available year-round but permits must be booked months in advance), or visiting penguin colonies in Antarctica (November to March). Always book well in advance, choose ethical and certified operators, follow responsible wildlife viewing guidelines, and never support attractions that exploit or harm animals.
