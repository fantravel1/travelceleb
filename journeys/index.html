How We Map Journeys

Every journey on TravelCeleb is meticulously reconstructed from publicly available creator content. Here is our process.

Public Content Analysis

We analyze publicly available YouTube videos, blog posts, and social media updates from creators. Every location is cross-referenced against video titles, descriptions, and visible landmarks.

Stop-by-Stop Verification

Each stop on a journey is verified against multiple content sources. We confirm dates, locations, and chronological order by comparing upload timelines and geo-tagged content.

Timeline Reconstruction

We reconstruct the chronological timeline of each journey using video publish dates, creator statements, and contextual clues. This allows us to show the true progression of each adventure.

Community Validation

Our mapped routes are reviewed by the travel community for accuracy. If a creator publicly corrects or updates their journey details, we update our records accordingly.
