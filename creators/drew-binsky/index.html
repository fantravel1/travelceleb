The Story of Drew Binsky

From a small-town Texas kid to the first content creator to set foot in every country on the planet.

Drew Binsky grew up in Dallas, Texas, where the idea of visiting all 197 countries on Earth was nothing more than a distant, almost impossible dream. After graduating from college in 2012, Drew made a decision that would forever alter the trajectory of his life: instead of following the conventional corporate career path, he packed his bags and moved to South Korea to teach English. It was there, surrounded by a culture vastly different from his own, that Drew caught what he calls "the travel bug" -- a deep, insatiable curiosity about the world and its people that would eventually drive him to the farthest corners of the globe.

What started as weekend trips around South Korea quickly escalated into month-long backpacking adventures across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and beyond. Drew began documenting his journeys on YouTube, but unlike many travel creators who focused on luxury hotels and picture-perfect sunsets, his content zeroed in on something different entirely: the people. His camera pointed at street vendors in Dhaka, shepherds in rural Afghanistan, students in North Korean classrooms, and families in the favelas of Brazil. His mission was simple but revolutionary in the travel content space -- show the "real" side of countries that Western media often reduced to headlines about conflict and poverty.

"Travel has taught me that people are good. No matter where you go in the world, no matter how different the culture, the language, or the religion, the vast majority of people you'll meet are kind, generous, and welcoming."

This philosophy became the backbone of Drew's channel and propelled his growth from a small travel vlogger into one of the most-watched travel creators on the planet. His videos about Pakistan, Iran, Iraq, Yemen, and Somalia challenged viewers to rethink their assumptions about these nations. Instead of danger and despair, audiences saw warmth, hospitality, rich culinary traditions, and vibrant communities. A single video about the "real Pakistan" accumulated tens of millions of views and fundamentally shifted how many of his viewers perceived the country.

In October 2019, Drew reached a milestone that cemented his place in travel history: he became the first travel content creator to visit every single country in the world -- all 197 internationally recognized nations. His final country was Saudi Arabia, and the emotional moment was captured in a video that went viral, racking up millions of views within days. The achievement was not just a personal triumph but a statement about what is possible when curiosity replaces fear and empathy replaces judgment.

Beyond the country count, Drew's impact extends into tangible humanitarian work. He launched a foundation dedicated to supporting education in developing countries, channeling the influence and resources from his platform into building schools and funding scholarships for children who might otherwise never have the opportunity to learn. In 2021, Drew married Deanna during their travels, and she has since become an integral part of his content and his life on the road. Together, they continue to travel, create, and inspire millions of people to look beyond borders and see the shared humanity that connects us all.

With over 5 million subscribers, 1,000+ videos, and more than 2 billion total views, Drew Binsky has built one of the most significant bodies of cultural travel content on the internet. His legacy is not measured in passport stamps alone but in the millions of minds he has opened and the stereotypes he has shattered, one country at a time.

The 197 Countries Journey

A decade-long odyssey from Dallas, Texas to every nation on the planet. Here are the key milestones along the way.

2012 - Dallas, Texas
The Beginning: College Graduation
Fresh out of college, Drew skipped the corporate track and bought a one-way ticket to South Korea to teach English. The first step of a journey that would span every country on Earth.
2013 - South Korea
Teaching English & Catching the Bug
Living in South Korea, Drew began exploring neighboring countries on weekends and holidays. The "travel bug" took hold permanently, and he knew there was no going back.
2014 - Southeast Asia
Backpacking Through Asia
From the temples of Cambodia to the street food stalls of Vietnam and the beaches of the Philippines, Drew immersed himself in Southeast Asian culture and began filming content.
2015 - Middle East & Central Asia
Challenging Media Narratives
Drew ventured into Iran, Iraq, and the Central Asian Stans. His videos showing warm hospitality and rich culture in these "misunderstood" regions became viral sensations.
2016 - Africa (East & West)
The African Continent
Traveling from Ethiopia to Nigeria, Kenya to Senegal, Drew documented the incredible diversity of African nations, reaching over 30 African countries across multiple trips.
2017 - South America & Pacific Islands
Crossing the Americas & the Pacific
From the Andes to the Amazon, Patagonia to the Caribbean, and then hopping across remote Pacific island nations like Tuvalu, Nauru, and Palau. The country count climbed past 150.
2017 - YouTube Milestone
1 Million Subscribers
Drew's channel crossed 1 million subscribers, fueled by his authentic cultural immersion content. His "Real Side of..." video series became a defining format in travel content.
2018 - Southern Africa & Oceania
Closing the Gaps
Drew tackled the hardest-to-reach nations: South Sudan, Eritrea, Equatorial Guinea, and remote island nations in the Indian Ocean. Each one brought unique stories and challenges.
2019 - The Final Push
Countries 190-196: The Home Stretch
With fewer than 10 countries remaining, each new visit became an event. Drew's audience followed along in real-time as he checked off Yemen, Somalia, Turkmenistan, and more.
Oct 2019 - Saudi Arabia
Country #197: The Final Destination
Drew set foot in Saudi Arabia as it opened to tourists for the first time, making it his 197th and final country. The emotional video documenting this milestone went viral, amassing millions of views.
2021 - Worldwide
Marriage & New Chapter
Drew married Deanna during their travels, beginning a new chapter. Together they continue to create content, revisit favorite countries, and support educational projects worldwide.
2024-Present
5M+ Subscribers & Continued Impact
With over 5 million subscribers and 2 billion views, Drew continues revisiting countries, deepening cultural stories, and running his foundation to support education in developing nations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know about Drew Binsky, his travels, and his content.

How many countries has Drew Binsky visited?
Drew Binsky has visited all 197 countries in the world, making him the first travel content creator to achieve this milestone. He reached his final country, Saudi Arabia, in October 2019 after approximately seven years of non-stop travel that began when he left Dallas, Texas in 2012.

Drew Binsky is originally from Dallas, Texas, USA. He grew up in a typical American household and attended university before deciding to forgo a traditional career path. After graduation in 2012, he moved to South Korea to teach English, which became the launching pad for his global travels and content creation career.
What was Drew Binsky's last country (197th)?
Drew's 197th and final country was Saudi Arabia, which he visited in October 2019. The emotional video documenting this milestone became one of his most-viewed pieces of content.

Is Drew Binsky married?
