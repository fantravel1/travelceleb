About Lost LeBlanc

Christian LeBlanc was working a corporate finance job in Canada when he made a decision that would change his life forever: he quit everything to travel the world.

What started as a gap year quickly transformed into a full-time career as one of YouTube's most influential travel creators. Under the name "Lost LeBlanc," Christian has built a channel of over 2.5 million subscribers by combining cinematic videography with honest, detailed reviews of hotels, destinations, and the digital nomad lifestyle.

Based primarily in Bali, Indonesia, Lost LeBlanc has become synonymous with luxury travel content in Southeast Asia. His hotel comparison videos (like "$1 vs $1,000,000 Hotel Room") have become a defining format in the travel YouTube genre, earning tens of millions of views. He's stayed at some of the world's most exclusive resorts - from Soneva Fushi in the Maldives to Aman Tokyo in Japan - providing detailed, honest reviews that help viewers understand what they're actually getting.

Beyond hotel reviews, Christian is a vocal advocate for the digital nomad lifestyle. He's created extensive content about building a location-independent business, the realities of long-term travel, and how to make money while exploring the world. His production company, "Jeala Media," also helps brands create travel content.

What sets Lost LeBlanc apart is his transparency. He openly discusses the costs, the challenges, and the behind-the-scenes reality of full-time travel. Whether he's reviewing a $30/night Bali villa or a $10,000/night overwater suite, his approach remains the same: honest, cinematic, and endlessly entertaining.

The Journey

From a cubicle in Canada to paradise in Bali.

2015 - Canada
The Decision to Quit
Left a corporate finance job to pursue full-time travel. Bought a one-way ticket to Southeast Asia with savings.
2016 - Thailand
First YouTube Videos
Started filming travel vlogs in Thailand. Early content focused on budget backpacking through Bangkok, Chiang Mai, and the islands.
2017 - Bali
Finding Home Base
Discovered Bali and made it his home base. Channel grew rapidly with Bali content - villa tours, digital nomad guides, and adventure vlogs.
2018 - Global
The Luxury Pivot
Started creating luxury hotel comparison content. "$1 vs $1,000,000 Hotel Room" format went viral, establishing his niche.
2019 - Maldives
1 Million Subscribers
Hit 1M subscribers while reviewing overwater villas in the Maldives. Became go-to creator for luxury resort content.
2021 - Bali
Building Jeala Media
Launched production company Jeala Media in Bali. Began helping brands and hotels create professional travel content.
2023-Present
2.5M+ Subscribers
Continues to review the world's best hotels and destinations. Expanded into real estate content showing luxury properties worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Lost LeBlanc?
Lost LeBlanc (real name Christian LeBlanc) is a Canadian luxury travel YouTuber with 2.5M+ subscribers. He quit his corporate finance job to travel the world and has become one of the most influential travel creators, specializing in hotel reviews, Bali content, and digital nomad lifestyle guides.
Where does Lost LeBlanc live?
Lost LeBlanc is primarily based in Bali, Indonesia, which has been his home base since 2017. He also spends significant time in Thailand and travels extensively for hotel reviews and destination content.
What hotels does Lost LeBlanc recommend?
Lost LeBlanc has reviewed hundreds of hotels worldwide. His top-rated properties include Soneva Fushi and Soneva Jani in the Maldives, Four Seasons Bali at Sayan, Aman Tokyo, various Viceroy and COMO properties, and boutique villas across Bali. He's known for his honest comparisons across all price points.
