About Sorelle Amore

Sorelle Amore is an Australian-born creator who redefined the art of self-portrait photography and turned her passion for travel into a movement for financial independence.

Known worldwide as the "Advanced Selfie" pioneer, Sorelle has built a community of over 1 million YouTube subscribers by teaching people how to take stunning self-portraits in breathtaking locations. Her technique goes far beyond the typical phone selfie -- combining professional camera setups, precise posing, natural lighting mastery, and an artist's eye for composition to create magazine-worthy images anywhere in the world.

After leaving Australia, Sorelle found her spiritual home in Iceland, where the dramatic volcanic landscapes, glaciers, and northern lights became the perfect canvas for her photography. She has since explored over 45 countries, with a particular focus on Nordic destinations including Norway, Greenland, and the Faroe Islands, capturing their raw, untouched beauty through her lens.

Beyond photography, Sorelle is a passionate advocate for financial freedom. She openly shares her income, investments, and strategies for building location-independent wealth, inspiring her audience to break free from conventional career paths. Her transparency about money has made her one of the most trusted voices in the creator economy.

Whether she is standing on the edge of an Icelandic glacier, driving through the fjords of Norway, or breaking down her monthly investment portfolio, Sorelle brings authenticity and artistry to everything she creates.

The Journey

From the Australian outback to the glaciers of Iceland.

2015 - Australia
Leaving Australia
Left her home in Australia with a camera and a dream. Began traveling internationally and experimenting with self-portrait photography in stunning landscapes around the world.
2016 - Global
Starting YouTube
Launched her YouTube channel to share photography tutorials and travel content. Her "Advanced Selfie" concept quickly resonated with audiences worldwide, gaining rapid traction.
2018 - Iceland
Moving to Iceland
Made the bold decision to relocate to Iceland, drawn by its otherworldly landscapes. The country became her creative playground and primary filming location for years to come.
2020 - Nordic
Nordic Exploration
Expanded her content to cover Norway, Greenland, and the Faroe Islands. Documented van life in Iceland and the realities of living in one of the most remote countries on earth.
2021 - Online
Financial Freedom Pivot
Began openly sharing her financial journey, investments, and income streams. Her transparent approach to money and freedom inspired a new wave of followers seeking independence.
2023-Present
1M+ Subscribers

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Sorelle Amore?
Sorelle Amore is an Australian-born travel creator, self-portrait photographer, and financial freedom advocate with over 1 million YouTube subscribers. She is known for pioneering the "Advanced Selfie" technique, creating stunning content from Iceland and Norway, and openly sharing her path to financial independence while traveling the world.
How does Sorelle Amore take her self-portraits?
Sorelle uses a professional camera on a tripod paired with a remote shutter release. She combines precise posing techniques, mastery of natural lighting, and an artist's eye for composition to create stunning self-portraits. She coined the term "Advanced Selfie" and has taught thousands through her YouTube tutorials and dedicated online courses.
Why did Sorelle Amore move to Iceland?
Sorelle was drawn to Iceland by its dramatic, otherworldly landscapes -- volcanic terrain, massive glaciers, powerful waterfalls, and the northern lights. She found the country to be the perfect backdrop for her self-portrait photography and fell in love with the raw, untouched nature that Iceland offers year-round.
What is Sorelle Amore's approach to financial freedom?
Sorelle advocates for building multiple income streams, smart investing, and minimizing unnecessary expenses. She transparently shares her earnings, investment portfolio, and financial strategies, encouraging her audience to pursue financial independence so they can live and travel on their own terms without being tied to a traditional job.
