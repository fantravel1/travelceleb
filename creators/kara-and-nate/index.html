The Story of Kara & Nate

From corporate cubicles in Nashville to 100+ countries around the world -- the story of how two dreamers turned a crazy idea into a life of adventure.

Kara and Nate Buchanan are a married couple from Nashville, Tennessee, who became two of the most beloved travel content creators on YouTube. Their story begins in 2016, when they made the bold decision to quit their comfortable corporate jobs, pack their bags, and set out on an ambitious mission: visit every country they could possibly reach, starting with a shoestring budget of just $30 per day. It was a leap of faith that would change their lives forever and inspire millions of viewers around the world to rethink what is possible.

The couple started their journey in Southeast Asia, backpacking through Thailand, Vietnam, and Cambodia while living on tight budgets and documenting every experience on their YouTube channel. In those early days, their videos were raw, unpolished, and refreshingly authentic -- a far cry from the glossy, overly produced travel content that dominated the platform. Viewers connected with their genuine excitement, their honest budget breakdowns, and the real dynamics of traveling as a couple. Kara's infectious enthusiasm and Nate's calm, analytical approach to planning created a perfect on-screen balance that felt like traveling with your best friends.

As their channel grew, so did their ambitions. They moved beyond Southeast Asia to explore Japan, India, Nepal, East Africa, South America, and Europe. Each region brought new challenges and incredible stories. They went gorilla trekking in Uganda, hiked through the stunning landscapes of Patagonia, road-tripped through 15 European countries in a single summer, and immersed themselves in cultures from the bustling streets of Delhi to the serene temples of Kyoto. Along the way, they developed a unique content style that blended budget travel tips with luxury splurges, proving that great travel experiences are not exclusively reserved for the wealthy.

The culmination of their early mission came in December 2019, when Kara and Nate reached their 100th country -- a milestone they had set out to achieve before turning 30. The moment was captured on YouTube and became one of their most-watched videos, a celebration not just of a number, but of the thousands of experiences, friendships, and personal transformations that led to that point. They had done it -- and their audience of millions had been along for the ride every step of the way.

When the global pandemic hit in 2020, Kara and Nate pivoted gracefully, turning their attention to exploring the United States and sharing domestic travel content that helped their audience discover the beauty in their own backyard. Their adaptability and positive outlook during uncertain times only deepened their connection with viewers. In the years since, they have welcomed a baby into their family, adding a beautiful new chapter to their journey. They continue to travel internationally with their growing family, now sharing insights on family travel, parenting on the road, and the joys of showing the world to their child. Based in Nashville but perpetually on the move, Kara and Nate remain pioneers of the travel couple genre -- authentic, relatable, and endlessly inspiring.

The 100-Country Journey

From their first one-way flight to 100+ countries across every continent -- the timeline of Kara and Nate's incredible travel odyssey.

January 2016
Left Nashville with One-Way Tickets
Kara and Nate quit their corporate jobs, sold most of their belongings, and booked one-way tickets out of Nashville. With $30/day budgets, savings, and a fledgling YouTube channel, they embarked on what they planned to be a year-long adventure. It would become something much bigger than either of them imagined.
March 2016
Southeast Asia: Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia
Their first major region. Living on $30/day, they backpacked through Bangkok's street food stalls, cruised Ha Long Bay, explored the temples of Angkor Wat, and fell in love with the backpacker lifestyle. These early videos, raw and unpolished, established their authentic, budget-friendly style that would attract millions of subscribers.
July 2016
Japan: First Luxury Experience
Japan marked a turning point as Kara and Nate discovered that travel does not have to be exclusively budget. From the neon streets of Tokyo to the serenity of Kyoto's temples and the culinary magic of Osaka, they began blending budget and luxury experiences -- a hallmark of their content ever since.
January 2017
India & Nepal: Cultural Immersion
Deeply immersive months in India and Nepal challenged and transformed them. From the chaos of Delhi and the beauty of the Taj Mahal to trekking in the Himalayas, this chapter pushed them far out of their comfort zone and produced some of their most emotionally resonant content.
August 2017
East Africa: Safari & Gorilla Trekking
East Africa delivered some of their most unforgettable experiences. Gorilla trekking in Uganda, witnessing the Great Migration in Tanzania, and exploring the vibrant culture of Kenya. These wildlife encounters became some of the most-watched videos on their channel and inspired countless viewers to visit Africa.
February 2018
South America: Patagonia to the Amazon
From the glaciers of Patagonia and hiking Torres del Paine to navigating the Amazon rainforest and exploring the ancient ruins of Machu Picchu, South America offered an extraordinary range of landscapes and adventures. Their detailed budget breakdowns for this region became go-to resources for aspiring travelers.
June 2018
Europe Road Trip: 15 Countries
An epic European road trip that covered 15 countries in a single stretch. From Portugal's Algarve coast to the fjords of Norway, from island-hopping in Greece to the fairy chimneys of Cappadocia. This series showcased their ability to make European travel accessible and affordable, shattering the myth that Europe is only for the wealthy.
December 2019
Country #100 Milestone
The moment millions of subscribers had been waiting for. Kara and Nate reached their 100th country, completing the audacious goal they had set for themselves. The celebratory video became one of their most-watched ever, a powerful testament to years of dedication, planning, and the transformative power of travel.
2020 - 2022
Pandemic Pivot & Domestic US Travel
When the world shut down, Kara and Nate adapted with grace. They explored the United States extensively, discovering national parks, hidden small towns, and domestic adventures that proved you do not need a passport to have extraordinary travel experiences. Their positive outlook during this period deepened their bond with their audience.
2023 - Present
Return to International Travel with Baby
A new chapter began as Kara and Nate returned to international travel with their baby. Now sharing the world through the eyes of new parents, they continue to explore new destinations while offering practical family travel advice. Their content has evolved beautifully, proving that having children does not mean the adventure has to stop.

What Makes Their Content Unique

Five pillars that set Kara and Nate apart in the crowded world of travel content creation.

💲

Budget Breakdowns in Every Video

One of the hallmarks of Kara and Nate's content is their transparent budget breakdowns. At the end of every trip, they share exactly how much they spent on flights, accommodation, food, and activities. This radical transparency helps viewers plan their own trips with realistic expectations, whether they are traveling on $30/day in Southeast Asia or splurging on a luxury resort.

💖

Authentic Couple Dynamics

Unlike many travel creators who present a polished, conflict-free image, Kara and Nate show the real dynamics of traveling as a couple. They share disagreements, travel mishaps, moments of frustration, and genuine joy. This authenticity has made them one of the most relatable couples on YouTube, and viewers often say watching their videos feels like traveling with close friends.

⚖️

Mix of Adventure and Luxury

Kara and Nate seamlessly blend budget backpacking with occasional luxury splurges. One video might feature a $5 hostel in Vietnam, while the next showcases a luxury overwater villa. This balance appeals to a wide range of viewers and shows that travel does not have to be one-dimensional. They prove that you can experience both ends of the spectrum on a single trip.

🌏

Cultural Immersion Focus

Beyond the usual tourist attractions, Kara and Nate prioritize genuine cultural immersion. They learn local phrases, eat at neighborhood restaurants, participate in traditional ceremonies, and build real connections with the people they meet. This approach gives viewers a deeper understanding of each destination beyond the surface-level highlights.

📚

Detailed Travel Tips & How-To Guides

Beyond entertainment, Kara and Nate provide genuinely useful travel information. From how to use credit card points for free flights to step-by-step visa guides, packing lists, and safety tips, their content serves as a practical travel education. Many viewers credit Kara and Nate with giving them the confidence and knowledge to plan their own first international trip.

👨‍👩‍👧

Evolving with Family Travel

As their family has grown, so has their content. Kara and Nate now share the unique challenges and joys of traveling with a baby, from navigating airports with a stroller to finding family-friendly activities in remote destinations. They are pioneering a new category of content that shows families they do not need to stop traveling when kids arrive.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know about Kara and Nate's travels, content, and personal life.

How many countries have Kara and Nate visited?
Kara and Nate have visited over 100 countries across all seven continents. Their goal was to visit 100 countries before turning 30, which they accomplished on schedule. Since then, they have continued adding new countries to their list and show no signs of slowing down, even with the addition of their baby to the travel team.

Their income streams include YouTube ad revenue from their channel with 4M+ subscribers and 800M+ total views, brand partnerships and sponsorships with travel companies, their merchandise store, and various digital products and business ventures. They have been transparent about earning a full-time living through travel content creation since approximately 2017, though they rarely share specific earnings figures publicly.
Where are Kara and Nate from?
Kara and Nate Buchanan are from Nashville, Tennessee, USA. They both grew up in the Nashville area and attended college there. After getting married, they worked corporate jobs in Nashville -- Kara in accounting and Nate in financial planning -- before quitting in January 2016 to travel full-time. Despite traveling to over 100 countries, they have maintained Nashville as their home base and currently live there between their international trips. Nashville remains a central part of their identity and they frequently feature the city in their content.
