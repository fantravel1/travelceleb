The Story of Louis Cole

From a camera-loving kid in Surrey to one of YouTube's most beloved travel vloggers and a pioneer of the daily vlog format.

Louis Cole grew up in Surrey, England, where his passion for filmmaking emerged at a young age. Before Fun For Louis became synonymous with adventure travel, Louis was already experimenting with video -- creating short films, sketches, and food challenges that would lay the foundation for one of YouTube's most enduring travel channels. In 2012, he launched his travel-focused channel and quickly found his voice: raw, authentic, and relentlessly positive. Viewers felt like they were right there with him on a tuk-tuk in India, surfing in Bali, or camping under the stars in the Sahara.

"Peace out, enjoy life, and live the adventure. Every single day is an opportunity to experience something incredible -- you just have to say yes."

In 2014, Louis embarked on his defining challenge: uploading a new vlog every single day. What started as a personal experiment turned into a legendary streak surpassing 1,000 consecutive daily uploads. The daily vlog format that Louis helped pioneer would go on to influence countless creators across YouTube. As his platform matured, so did his mission -- he became a vocal advocate for sustainable travel, using his reach to spotlight conservation efforts and eco-friendly practices. With over 2 million subscribers and a legacy spanning more than a decade, Fun For Louis remains one of the most authentic voices in the travel creator space.

Signature Destinations

The places that shaped Louis's journey and produced some of Fun For Louis's most memorable content.

India

From the chaos of Delhi to the backwaters of Kerala, India became one of Louis's most-revisited destinations -- a sensory overload of color, flavor, and warmth that fueled dozens of vlogs.

Peru

Trekking to Machu Picchu, exploring the Amazon basin, and connecting with indigenous communities -- Peru offered Louis the perfect blend of adventure and cultural depth.

Indonesia

Surfing in Bali, diving in Komodo, and exploring the volcanic landscapes of Java. Indonesia's natural diversity made it a recurring backdrop for Louis's most cinematic content.

Scotland

Road-tripping through the Scottish Highlands, camping beside misty lochs, and embracing the rugged beauty of his home region. Scotland represented Louis's connection to his British roots.

Morocco

Sleeping under the stars in Saharan desert camps, getting lost in the medinas of Marrakech, and savoring traditional tagines. Morocco delivered adventure at every turn.

Japan

From the neon-lit streets of Tokyo to the tranquil temples of Kyoto, Japan's striking contrasts between ancient tradition and futuristic innovation captivated Louis and his audience.

The Fun For Louis Journey

From early YouTube experiments to becoming one of the platform's most iconic travel vloggers.

2012 - London, England
Launching the YouTube Channel
Louis Cole launched Fun For Louis on YouTube, blending food challenges, stunts, and early travel content. His raw energy and fearless approach quickly attracted a loyal following and set the stage for what was to come.
2014 - Worldwide
The Daily Vlog Challenge Begins
Louis committed to uploading a vlog every single day -- no exceptions. This daily cadence transformed his channel into a must-watch destination and pioneered a format that countless travel creators would later adopt.
2016 - Global
1,000 Consecutive Daily Vlogs
After nearly three unbroken years, Louis crossed the 1,000 daily vlog milestone. The achievement cemented Fun For Louis as one of the most dedicated creators on YouTube and proved that consistency and authenticity could build an empire.
2018 - Present
Sustainability & Purposeful Travel
Louis shifted his focus toward sustainability advocacy, spotlighting ocean conservation, eco-friendly communities, and responsible travel practices. His content evolved from pure adventure to adventure with purpose, inspiring millions to travel more consciously.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know about Fun For Louis, daily vlogging, and Louis Cole's travel philosophy.

How many consecutive daily vlogs did Fun For Louis upload?
Louis Cole uploaded over 1,000 consecutive daily vlogs on his Fun For Louis YouTube channel. The streak began in 2014 and ran for nearly three years without missing a single day. Each vlog captured a new adventure, destination, or experience, making it one of the most ambitious sustained content commitments in YouTube history.
What are Fun For Louis's best travel tips?
Louis is a firm believer in saying yes to new experiences and traveling with an open mind. His top advice includes staying in hostels to meet fellow travelers, using local transportation for authentic experiences, eating street food to connect with local culture, and keeping a flexible itinerary. He also emphasizes sustainable travel -- respecting local environments, minimizing waste, and supporting local businesses.
Why did Fun For Louis stop daily vlogging?
After surpassing 1,000 consecutive daily vlogs, Louis stepped back from the daily schedule to focus on higher quality, more intentional content. He has spoken openly about the mental and physical demands of daily uploads and recognized that quality storytelling sometimes requires more time. His content since then has focused on sustainability, long-form adventure series, and purposeful travel.
How many countries has Fun For Louis visited?
Fun For Louis has traveled to over 80 countries across six continents. His travels range from well-trodden destinations like Japan and Indonesia to off-the-beaten-path adventures in the Amazon rainforest, the Sahara Desert, and remote Scottish highlands. Louis continues to add new destinations, always prioritizing authentic experiences over simply checking off countries.
